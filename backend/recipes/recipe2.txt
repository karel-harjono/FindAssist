Fluffy pancakes
These will be the best and fluffiest pancakes that you will ever taste in your life.
Here is how you make them:

Servings
2

Ingredients
2 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup flour
2/3 cup milk

1. Beat the eggwhite until cream like.
2. Mix everything together really well.
3. Fry until golden brown.


Chocolate chip cookie
Who wants grandma's secret recipe for perfect cookies?
Here is how you make them:

Servings:	18 cookies

Ingredients
8 tablespoons soft butter
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup sugar
1 egg
1 cup flour
1 cup chocolate chips
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt	18 cookies

1. Mix butter and both sugars.
2. Break the egg into the bowl.
3. Stir in the rest of the ingredients.
4. Put the mixture on a cookie sheet.
5. Bake the cookies for 10 to 12 minutes until golden brown.


Tomato soup
The perfect way to warm yourself up on a cold winter day.
Here is how you make them:

Servings
2

Ingredients
4 diced tomatoes (undrained)
4 tablespoons butter
10 sausages
1 cup heavy cream
1 serving of noodles
1/2 cup water

1. Simmer the tomatoes with butter until you have a smooth saucy mixture.
2. Put in water and cream.
3. Put in the sausages.
4. Bring to a boil.
5. Simmer for 10 minutes.
